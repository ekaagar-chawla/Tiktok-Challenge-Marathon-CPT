Instructions for Tiktok Challenge Marathon.

Welcome to the Tiktok Challenge Marathon by Ekaagar and Aaryan.
The game is quite simple, but also very fun. You've three different challenges, which you've to beat in the least amount of time possible.
You will have multiple attempts to beat this challenge. Invite your friends and family too.
The three games are :
1. Swap the Bottles
2. Hit the Pins
3. Solve the puzzle
Good luck!
